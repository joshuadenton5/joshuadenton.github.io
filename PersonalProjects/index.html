Cooking With Polygons - Casual Cooking Sim - Published Online

In Cooking With Polygons, you are in charge of preparing meals in a busy resturant kitchen, although you are the only chef. Earn money by creating top quality meals for the ever picky customers. Expect your work to be scrutinised and get rewarded hansomely for your hard work. After earning enough money, you can upgrade your status by learning new recipies to add to your portfolio.

Initally a university project, I decided to keeping updating this game. I am the sole developer, responsible for developing most of the assets (including the models - basic I know). This is certainlly the project I have put the most time and energy into.
